Who is behind Origin Galway

Origin Galway was set up by William Coppinger(right) & Kieran Farrell(left) in March 2015. There was a clear lack of techno in the mid-week student scene which they looked to fill. After theyre first gig in Carbon Nightclub the club night has been going from strenght to strength since it has started and now plays in many locations around the city including the two biggest & busiest clubs.

Although Origin Galway main focus was to bring techno to students mid-week. Lately they have started doing more weekend gigs and playing more than just techno. With gigs in the Roisin Dubh and the Blue Note in Galways "Westend" playing House, Funk and Disco.
